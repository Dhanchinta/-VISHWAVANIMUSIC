Beginner Class

This beginner level class is perfect for first-time musicians. Students will learn the basics of Vocal or Veena instrument music theory.

Intermediate Class

The intermediate class builds off of the skills acquired in the beginner class. Students will continue working with their Vocal and/or Veena instrument

Advanced Class

The advanced class is for students who have been playing consistently for over 5 years.

Vanisri learned Classical Vocal & Veena from her mother Smt.N.Seshamma ,a veena player and vocalist. later she had the privileage of learning music from the lengendary Smt.Malathi Padmanadha Rao for several years. Vanisri hails from the musical heritage family of Sri NC Parthasarthy Iyengar, author of the premier book on classical music,Ganakalabodhini I have been teaching in the Pacific North West since the last 15+ years I am a strong believer that everyone should be able to have a life of music. That's why I became a teacher. Whether you're a just a beginner or an advanced student needing guidance, let's explore music together I offer both group and private lessons, separated by skill levels and experience in both Vocal as well as Veena classes.
